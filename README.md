# SOPIP
The codes provided here are corresponding to the numerical simulations in our paper, J. Wang, M. Dong, B. Liang, G. Boudreau, and H. Abou-Zeid, "Hierarchical Semi-Online Optimization for Cooperative MIMO Networks with Information Parsing", submitted to IEEE Transactions on Wireless Communications, 2022. For any further development, please kindly cite our paper.
